About Us

Ford Pickup Cambodia is one of the leading specialists in high-performance pickup trucks in Cambodia. We are dedicated to providing customers with premium-quality Ford vehicles, including the Ford Ranger Raptor, Wildtrak, XLT, XLS, and the powerful F-150 series. Every vehicle is carefully selected and designed to meet the needs of Cambodian drivers, whether for personal use, business, construction, agriculture, or off-road adventures. Our goal is to deliver not only reliable vehicles but also outstanding customer service that builds long-term trust and satisfaction. At Ford Pickup Cambodia, we believe that buying a vehicle is more than just making a purchase—it is an investment in quality, safety, and performance. Therefore, we ensure that every customer receives professional consultation, transparent pricing, and detailed information before making a decision. Our experienced sales team is always ready to help customers choose the best pickup truck according to their budget and lifestyle. We also provide comprehensive after-sales services, including vehicle maintenance, genuine Ford spare parts, warranty support, repair services, and technical assistance by skilled and experienced technicians. Customer satisfaction is our highest priority, and we continuously improve our services to ensure every customer enjoys a smooth and worry-free ownership experience. Our commitment is to become the most trusted Ford pickup dealership in Cambodia by offering high-quality vehicles, competitive prices, flexible financing options, and exceptional customer care.
